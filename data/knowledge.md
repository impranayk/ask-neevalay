# Neevalay Tots — Knowledge Base

## About Neevalay Tots
Neevalay Tots is a research-driven, global preschool in New Delhi with the promise
"Nurturing Roots, Shaping the Future." The name comes from the Sanskrit words
*Neev* (foundation) and *Alay* (home) — a home where strong foundations are built.
We believe strong foundations shape strong futures, and our goal is to raise
confident thinkers, creators and communicators — not children who simply memorise.

## Our Approach and Philosophy
Our approach is child-centric and research-driven, inspired by the Montessori and
Reggio Emilia methodologies. Learning is playful, hands-on and led by each child's
natural curiosity, within a warm and emotionally safe environment. Our work rests
on six pillars:
- Research-Driven — practices grounded in early-childhood research
- Emotionally Nurturing — a caring, secure space where every child feels valued
- Global Pedagogy — internationally informed early-learning methods
- Child-Centric — learning paced to each child's development
- Technology-Integrated — thoughtful, age-appropriate use of technology
- Professionally Managed — run with structure, care and accountability

## Programmes and Age Groups
Neevalay Tots offers programmes for children from about 2 to 8 years:
- Playgroup — ages 2 to 3 years
- Nursery — ages 3 to 4 years
- Kindergarten 1 (KG1) — ages 4 to 5 years
- Kindergarten 2 (KG2) — ages 5 to 6 years
- Daycare — flexible care for ages 2 to 6 years
- Enrichment Programmes — ages 3 to 8 years
The best way to choose the right programme for your child is to speak with our team
or visit us — we're happy to guide you.

## A Nurturing Day
Days at Neevalay blend play, exploration and gentle routine. Children learn through
hands-on activities, stories, music, movement and free play, supported by caring
educators who follow each child's pace. To experience a typical day and our
classrooms first-hand, we warmly invite you to visit us.

## Settling In and Common Questions
Starting preschool is a big step, and we support every family through it:
- Separation anxiety is completely normal — our educators gently help new children
  settle in at their own pace.
- We partner closely with parents and keep you updated about your child's day.
- For questions about toilet training, meals, nap time, a child who is unwell, or
  anything specific to your child, our team is happy to talk it through with you.
If your child has a specific health condition or need, please speak with our team
directly so we can support them well.
