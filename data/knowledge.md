## Timings and daily hours
Neevalay Tots is open **Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM**. Half-day and
full-day options are available across our programmes, and daycare extends through
the day for working parents. For the exact schedule that suits your child's age
and your routine, our team will gladly walk you through the options — reach us on
WhatsApp or book a visit.

## Programmes and ages
We welcome children from about 2 to 8 years across a play-based, developmentally
staged path:
- **Playgroup** — 2 to 3 years
- **Nursery** — 3 to 4 years
- **Kindergarten (KG1 & KG2)** — 4 to 6 years
- **Daycare** — full-day care for 2 to 6 years
- **Enrichment** — specialised programmes for 3 to 8 years
Each stage is designed around the child's developmental needs, blending structured
learning with joyful, hands-on play.

## Our teaching approach
Neevalay's pedagogy is **child-centric, research-driven and play-based**, inspired
by the **Montessori and Reggio Emilia** methods and aligned to India's National
Curriculum Framework for the Foundational Stage (**NCF-FS 2022**) and **NEP 2020**.
We nurture the whole child — cognitive, language, social-emotional, physical and
creative development — through exploration, curiosity and warm, individual
attention, rather than rote learning.

## How to book a visit
The best way to experience Neevalay is to visit us. You can **book a campus visit**
through our enquiry form, message us on **WhatsApp**, or call our team — we'll find
a convenient time and show you around. A visit lets you see the classrooms, meet
our educators, and ask everything you'd like about your child's day with us.

## How admissions work
To apply, you can fill in our **admission form** online, and our team will guide
you through the next steps — a campus visit, a warm interaction with your child,
and confirming a place. We're happy to explain the whole process on a call or over
WhatsApp so it feels simple and unhurried.
